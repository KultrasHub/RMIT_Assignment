Overview
    My project idea is about video game, it is a 2D mobile open world game. The elementary concept is similar to "Hungry Shark where players move freely and eat others fish to survive and fill gold rush bar. The difference is the project's theme is about sci-fi, and players control their ship in space. Therefore, the play style is shifted as space ship could be upgrade and players would scout planets and destroy bots' spaceship to get the components for upgradation. The most fun part which is going frenzy after stacking enough power.
Motivation
    Statisitcally, Hungry Shark is a successful game and highly rated. Additionally, Scifi the a favourable and marvelous theme, it plays a role of envisionment the future. With the shortage of scifi games in the market, it is also easier to get attention from the community. The hook of the game is developed from the Hungry Shark, which are exploration and the gold frenzy. Moreover, getting items and combining them are also an interesting consideration, normally people enjoy being crafty, therefore, the items combination will be attractive to people.
Description
    Starting with the story, after reading guliver's travel, a young children wondered if he could ever find a tiny island. Following his father's suggestion, "some where in the outer space", he then set his new dream. For many years later, the young man become a famous astronaut, and here he spaces on. The games surround this character, his crews and AI. Throughout the game, players experience a liveful space, with some iconic and exotic region of the universe, the blackhole, the white hole, neutron stars, and beyond our knowledge. There will be the presence of megastructure such as the Dyson sphere, the ring world and dramatically, life in the void emerges here. To create a more diversity of environment in space, the twelves zodiac is divided in two groups: Celestial and Dark space or know as Corrupted celestial. Another iconic feature of the game is item combination, players would find random pieces of equipment all over the space. By scanning planets, destroying enemies's ship, items would randomly drop from there. There are four categories of equipment: Attachor, Utility, Offence and Defence. The combination system is simple, the main ship and attachor equipment are in square shape, which have four sides. Other non-attachor components must be attached to the main ship or attachors. For every non-attachor component to be attached, it requires one available side of the main ship of attachor. Here is how it works:
    The open map is divided into small regions, and will be loaded or unloaded by trigger method. This increases performance and quality of the game. The feature that is kept from HungryShark is the gold frenzy. In detail, after finishing killing an enemy or scanning a planet players receive an amount of energy, at full charge, gold frenzy is activated. During this process, the spaceship is immune to any damage and gains adaptive power to attack one-level-higher enemies. This burst of energy runs out but will recharge by killing enemies and scanning. This feature boosts speed of the game. The second frenzy is megafrenzy, the spaceship grows in size, and power, score and gold earned would multiply during this frenzy, this will satisfy the hunger of hunting large enemies of low level players.
    In the shop, players would buy higher-quality ships, which have higher level limits, high capacity and more attractive appearance. The main currency is golden coin, which is called gold, it is earned from playing. The second accepted currency is diamond earned from purchasing by real money or rarely dropped from monsters in games. As diamond rarely appeared, they are valuable and can be used in exchange for amazing subsidary equipment such as skins, decorations and loots. To increase the level of exploration, adding treasures and hidden paths inside the game would be a consideration. Treasure works similar to loots but different items. In term of monetization, players will also be able watch rewards ads to boost the game process faster.
